About Me

Greetings! I'm thrilled to share my journey with you. My name is Daniel Mulenga, and I hail from the beautiful country of Zambia. My academic and professional adventures have taken me across continents, shaping me into the individual I am today.

My Studies:

I'm currently enrolled in a diploma program at North Metropolitan TAFE in Perth, Western Australia. It's been quite a journey adapting to a new place and a different education system, but it's been an exciting chapter of growth and learning.

Web Development Adventure:

Right now, I'm really into web development. It's like creating digital art for me. I enjoy the challenge of coding and making things work on the internet. It's not just about websites; it's about solving puzzles and bringing ideas to life.

Beyond Books:

When I'm not studying, I love exploring new places. Traveling is a passion of mine, and I always carry my camera to capture the beauty of the places I visit. I also enjoy movies and music; they're my go-to for relaxation and entertainment.

More Than Just Code:

Even though I spend a lot of time with computers and coding, there's a lot more to who I am. I believe in finding a balance between the digital world and real-life experiences. My love for technology coexists with my love for travel, photography, movies, and music.
